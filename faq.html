Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our templates and services

General Questions

TempletNest is a marketplace for high-quality website templates built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We provide developers and designers with ready-to-use templates that can be easily customized for various purposes.

Purchasing a template is simple. Browse our collection, select the template you like, click "Buy Now," and follow the checkout process. After payment, you'll receive a download link and license key via email.

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact our support team with your order details for a full refund, as long as the template hasn't been significantly modified or used in a live project.

Template Usage

All our templates come with well-documented code and clear instructions. You can customize colors, fonts, content, and functionality by editing the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files. Each template includes a documentation file with specific guidance.

Yes, all our templates are fully responsive and designed to work seamlessly across all devices, from mobile phones to desktop computers. They use modern CSS techniques like flexbox and grid to ensure optimal display on any screen size.

Our templates are built with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. Some templates may also include additional libraries or frameworks like Bootstrap, Font Awesome, or jQuery, depending on the specific template's requirements and complexity.

Licensing

All premium templates come with a standard license that allows you to use the template for a single website or application. For multiple projects or commercial use, you may need to purchase an extended license. Each template's license terms are clearly outlined on its product page.

Yes, with a standard license, you can use our templates for client projects. You're allowed to modify the template to create a unique end product for your client. However, you cannot resell or redistribute the template as-is or with minimal changes.

A standard license allows you to use the template for one website or application. An extended license is required if you want to use the template for multiple projects, for a SaaS application, or if you're building a template that will be resold to others. Extended licenses typically cost 5-10 times the standard license price.

Support

We offer free email support for all template purchases. Our team can help with questions about template installation, basic customization, and functionality issues. For advanced development assistance, we recommend hiring a developer or using our premium support option.

You have access to support for 6 months from the date of purchase. After this period, you can still access our knowledge base and community forums. Premium support plans are available for extended assistance.

Yes, we offer professional customization services for our templates. Our team can help you modify templates to better suit your specific needs. Contact our sales team for a quote on custom work. We also have a directory of vetted freelance developers who specialize in our templates.

Yes, we offer volume discounts for bulk purchases. If you're interested in purchasing multiple templates, contact our sales team for a custom quote. We also have an annual subscription plan that provides access to all templates at a reduced rate.
